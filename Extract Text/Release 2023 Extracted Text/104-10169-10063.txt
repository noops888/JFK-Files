SUBJECT: David Graham McCANS aka David G. DUBOIS
1. Reference is made to your memorandum dated 19 October 1965
in which you request information concerning the current activities
and whereabouts of David G. DUBOIS. We have learned from a re-
liable source that since the time DUBOIS came to Cairo he has been
employed for the Middle East News Agency, the Arab Observer magazine
and since 1964 the advertising firm of Hamilton Wright. Although
it cannot be confirmed, the source of this information is certain
that Subject is still in Cairo.
2. DUBOIS has also stated to our source that he may marry an
Egyptian, who is not further identified.
