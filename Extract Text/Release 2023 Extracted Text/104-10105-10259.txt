1. This memorandum is for your information only.
2. Mr. Arthur Briggs Hanson, head of the law firm, Hanson,
O'Brien, Birney and Stickle of Washington, D. C., has cut short
his vacation plans and returned to Washington to assist in the
defense of E. Howard Hunt in the Watergate 5 case.

providing cover for a staff agent who is stationed in Athens
and working on Arab and African targets. To date, this cover
placement has presented no operational security problems. CCS
has no information that Howard Hunt knows of Mr. Hanson's
cooperation with CIA.
