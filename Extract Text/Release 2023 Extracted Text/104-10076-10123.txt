DURING THE THIRD WEEK OF NOVEMBER 1963 DR. JACQUE FOURCAND,
THE PERSONAL PHYSICIAN OF HAITIAN PRESIDENT FRANCOIS DUVALIER, ARRIVED
THREE HOURS LATE AT A MEETING HE WAS TO HAVE WITH SEVERAL BUSINESSMEN
IN PORT AU PRINCE. FOURCAND SAID HE WAS DELAYED BECAUSE HIS ASSISTANCE
WAS REQUIRED IN TREATING DUVALIER WHO HAD SUFFERED A SLIGHT HEART
SEIZURE THAT EVENING. (SOURCE COMMENT: FOURCAND IS THE ONLY
PHYSICIAN PERMITTED TO ATTEND DUVALIER. HE IS AN ANTI-AMERICAN LEFTIST
WHO IS REWARDED FOR HIS SERVICES TO DUVALIER BY PARTICIPATING IN BUSINESS
VENTURES IN HAITI ON BEHALF OF DUVALIER WITH WHOM HE SHARES PROFITS
ACCORDING TO DUVALIER'S REQUIREMENTS.)
