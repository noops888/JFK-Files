In accordance with a Resolution of the Cuban Foreign Minister,
Cuban Foreign Service Auxiliaries were regraded on the following new
scale as from 1st January, 19638-
2.
Fourth Grade Auxiliary with an annual basic salary of
US dollars 2,568, equivalent to the former grade
of First Class Auxiliary;
Third Grade Auxiliary with an anhual basic salary of
US dollars 2,340, equivalent to the fomer grade
of Second Class Auxiliary;
Second Grade Auxiliary with an annual basic salary of
US dollars 2,052, equivalent to the former grade
of Third Class Auxiliary;
First Grade Auxiliary with an annual basio salary of
US dollars 1,716 (new grade).
