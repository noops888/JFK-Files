1. WHILE IN NEW YORK, 7 OCTOBER CONTACTING CUBAN EXILES, WITH
POSSIBLE LINKS MEXI, LITAMIL-3 HAD CHANCE ENCOUNTER AND CHATTED
PRIVATELY WITH ANTONIO DE SOUZA CARBAJAL, MEMBER CUBAN IN DELEGATION.
ON OWN INITIATIVE, L-3 SUGGESTED DEFECTION TO WHICH DE SOUZA
REPLIED HE MERELY PERFORMING HIS DUTY AND VERY FRIGHTENED. DE
SOUZA DID STATE HE WOULD TALK WITH L-3 AGAIN IN FUTURE UNDER
SECURE CIRCUMSTANCES. L-3 DESCRIBES DE SOUZA AS A KEY MAN.
2. REQUEST TRACES, COMMENTS, ADVICE ON ANOTHER TRIP BY L-3 TO
N.Y. FOR PURPOSE TALKING DE SOUZA.
