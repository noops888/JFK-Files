II. DESCRIPTION OF AREA
The SUBJECT SITE building is located in a
modern, middle-class apartment development known as
(24 Summit Hills Apartments) which is located along a
thoroughfare of Washington, D.C., 16th Street and
Route 24 (East-West Highway). The SUBJECT SITE building
is located in Montgomery County, Maryland. To the south
of the SUBJECT SITE building is another apartment building
known as (24 Summit Hills Apartments) which is located
approximately 1/2 mile south of the SUBJECT SITE
building. 16th Street and the SUBJECT SITE building
is located in a large parking lot. Access to this parking
lot, which is parallel to the east wing of the SUBJECT
SITE building, can be gained from either 16th Street
proceeding in a westerly direction or from East-West
Highway, which has an entrance to the parking lot. To
the west and north of SUBJECT SITE building are additional
apartment buildings. The area is laid out in a grid pattern
with wings extending north, south, east, and west.
SUBJECT SITE is located in the extreme
southeast corner of the east wing of SUBJECT SITE building.
D.C. Transit bus service is available along East-West
Highway and at the parking lot located in close proximity
to SUBJECT SITE. A bus stop for D.C. Transit. The
parking lot is located directly in front of the east wing
of SUBJECT SITE building. Taxicab service is also
available. Complete personal services are located
approximately 1 mile away in Silver Spring, Maryland,
which can be reached by traveling east on Route 10. A
mile of parking area for automobiles surrounds the SUBJECT
SITE building.
